About Us

Basti Ki Pathshala is a grassroots initiative dedicated to transforming the lives of children living in urban slums. Our mission is to bridge the educational gap by providing free, quality learning opportunities to those who need it most. We believe that every child, regardless of their background, deserves access to education, nutrition, and a safe environment to grow and dream.

Our passionate team of volunteers runs community learning centers where children receive academic support, life skills training, and emotional guidance. We also organize health camps, distribute nutritious meals, and conduct awareness drives for parents and the community. Through our efforts, we aim to empower children to break the cycle of poverty and build a brighter future for themselves and their families.

Since our inception, we have impacted thousands of lives by not only focusing on education but also on holistic development. We invite you to join us as a volunteer, supporter, or donor and become a part of this journey of hope and change.
